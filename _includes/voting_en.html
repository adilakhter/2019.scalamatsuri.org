About voting

Here is the general general structure for voting:

  • Sessions are voted by early bird ticket holders, sponsors, and staff.
  • The voting will be done for the following categories separately: 40-minute talks in English, 40-minute talks in Japanese, 15-minute talks in English, and 15-minute talks in Japanese.
  • We will try to select eight 40-minute talks and three 15-minute talks for both the languages. The number may vary depending on the travel budgets.
  • Only one session will be offered per person. (You're also welcome to give the second one at the unconference in day 2)
If you decide to submit multiple talks, we recommend submitting 40-minute and 15-minute talks to avoid splitting your votes.

While the CFP is open, we'll accept cancellation or modification to the proposal. For the change or any other questions, please contact us using this inquiry form. We might also contact you to ask about the session or ask to modify the content.
